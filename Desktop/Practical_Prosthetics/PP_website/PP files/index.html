About us

Practical Prosthetics integrates 3D printing in the prosthetic manufacturing process, developing functional prosthetics operated by an independent and intuitive control system. Featured Article

Product

Affordability

By 3D printing our functional prosthetics, we greatly reduce the cost of production, shipping, and maintenance.

Functionality

Our intuitive ring control system reduces the average learning curve for a functional prosthetic to a few days while maintaining accessibiliy to a manifold of settings.

The Fit

Our prosthetics are not dependent on the location of the amputation. Using 3D imaging software, our techonolgy can scale and adapt to each unique case.
